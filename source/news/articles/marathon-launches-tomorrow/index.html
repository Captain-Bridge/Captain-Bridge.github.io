Marathon Launches Tomorrow

Celebrate with a new cinematic trailer and prepare to infil with our launch FAQ

Marathon Dev Team March 4, 2026
community1057 words5 min read

Prepare to shed your mortal form and embrace death as a cybernetic mercenary — your future on Tau Ceti awaits.

...if you escape, will they call you god?

Marathon's launch cinematic trailer features new music written by Ryan Lott & Son Lux, with vocals and lyrics by Poppy.

You can listen to "In Death We've Just Begun" on all major music streaming platforms. The full Marathon OST releases on March 6.

Marathon Launches on March 5

Marathon goes live worldwide tomorrow March 5 at 10 AM PT on Steam, PlayStation 5, and Xbox Series X|S. We’re releasing Marathon globally at the same time — here's an image to help you find the release time in your region.

 

Marathon Launch FAQ

As we venture forward into day one of Marathon, here’s an FAQ to make sure you’re prepared:

When does Marathon launch?

Servers go live tomorrow March 5 at 10 AM PT. Check the regional launch times graphic above for your local start time!

Can I pre-load Marathon?

Yes. Pre-loading is available now on all platforms. Make sure to check your storefront of choice to ensure you're ready to go.

Do I need to download the game again if I played the Server Slam?

Yes. The launch build is different from the Server Slam build. Redownload Marathon from your platform’s store.

Which platforms can I play Marathon on?

You can play on Steam (PC), PlayStation 5, and Xbox Series X|S with full cross-play and cross-save. You will need an active online subscription on console to play.

What is different from the Server Slam?

Launch includes Marathon’s full progression, a new Runner shell, Outpost, and more:

  • Full faction and contract progression
    • All six factions, including Sekiguchi Genetics
  • All six launch Runner shells, including Thief
  • Three zones, including Outpost
    • Outpost will unlock the day after launch, giving the community time to gear up and unlock the zone at Runner Level 12
  • 28 weapons, plus mods, implants, cores, and more to craft your perfect builds
  • Earnable cosmetics, achievements, and storytelling through the Codex

Launch also includes fixes and updates informed by the Server Slam, including adjustments for PvP frequency on Perimeter (Beginner), with more changes to come. Read more about what we heard in the Server Slam here.

When do Outpost, Cryo Archive, and Ranked mode go live?

  • Outpost will be available the day after launch and will require Runner Level 12. This gives you a bit of time to gear up before you go headlong into one of our more treacherous zones.
  • Ranked mode will go live in the second half of March.
  • Cryo Archive will go live once you unlock the way into orbit in the second half of March.

I played the Server Slam. When will I get my rewards and do they expire?

Server Slam cosmetic rewards and Arrival Caches will roll out during the first day of launch. It will take some time for them to make it to everyone’s in-game Mailboxes, so if you don’t see them upon logging in hold tight — they should arrive soon.

If you earned Server Slam rewards, you must log in before Season 1 concludes to receive them. If you do not log in during Season 1, you will not receive your rewards.

Need a reminder of what tier of Arrival Cache loot you secured in the Slam? Head here.

When will I get my Twitch Drops from watching during the Server Slam?

If you tuned in to Marathon on Twitch and secured Twitch Drops, your rewards will be granted to your Bungie.net profile and delivered in-game at launch on March 5. The Twitch Sponsored Kit may take up to 48 hours to arrive in your in-game Mailbox and must be redeemed by March 31.

Will there be new Twitch Drops at launch? How do I earn them?

Yes! To earn Twitch Drops, make sure you...

  • Link your Bungie.net account to your Twitch account
  • Watch Marathon streams in the Twitch Directory with Drops enabled during the launch window
  • Claim each reward from your Twitch Inventory, then log in to Marathon to receive them

There are six rewards up for grabs during the launch weekend. Tune in from March 5 at 10 AM PT to March 9 at 10 AM PT to secure:

  • Weapon Sticker: Smol K.i.t.t.y. (subscribe or gift a subscription to a creator playing Marathon)
  • Twitch Sponsored Kit, aka Standard in-game loot (watch for 1 hour)
  • Profile Emblem: Pink Graffiti Kitty (watch for 1 hour)
  • Profile Background: Pink Graffiti Kitty (watch for 2 hours)
  • Weapon Sticker: Pink Graffiti Kitty (watch for 3 hours)
  • Weapon Skin (Style): White and Purple – Ballistic Sniper (watch for 4 hours)

You’ll also be able to earn a new set of Twitch Drops during the Tau Ceti Cup on March 14, an event pitting creator teams against each other to prove they’re the best Runners on Tau Ceti. More details to come!

How do I claim my platform's weapon charms? What are they?

Players on PlayStation, Steam, and Xbox will all receive weapon charms unique to their platform.

Steam players will automatically receive the Crowbar weapon charm upon logging in at launch.

PlayStation players receive the For Freedom, Battle Guitar, and Onryō weapon charms. Visit the PlayStation® Store when Marathon releases on March 5 to claim your rewards.

Xbox players will automatically receive the Emerald Clutch and Emerald Catch weapon charms upon logging in at launch.

Will there be login queues occur at launch? How do I check server status?

Launch days are busy! Brief queues or updates may occur, and we’ll communicate status if we need to perform maintenance via the BungieServerStatus Twitter and Bluesky accounts. We’ll also share updates in our community Discord.

How do seasons work? Where can I see what’s coming next?

Head over to our Seasons in Marathon post for a full rundown, including some teases for what’s cooking in Season 2.

How do I get help or report an issue?

  • Visit the Marathon Help Center for troubleshooting and known issues. If you run into reward delivery problems, account linking errors, or crashes, this is a good place to start.
  • You can also join the official Marathon Discord and follow the MarathonDevTeam Twitter for alerts or to ask for help.

Tomorrow is the day — we'll see you on Tau Ceti soon. 🐛

你可以在各大主流音乐平台收听单曲《In Death We've Just Begun》。《失落星船：马拉松》完整原声带将于3月7日发布。

《失落星船：马拉松》将于北京时间3月6日凌晨2点全球上线，登陆Steam、PlayStation 5及Xbox Series X|S平台。我们将在全球同一时间发布游戏。查看下图，即可找到你所在地区的发布时间。

 

《失落星船：马拉松》发布常见问题解答

值此《失落星船：马拉松》启程之日，附上常见问题解答一份，助你整装待发：

《失落星船：马拉松》何时发布？

服务器将于北京时间3月6日凌晨2点开启。请查阅上方各地区发布时间图示，确认你所在地区的发布时间！

可以预载吗？

可以。目前所有平台均已开放预载。请前往你选择的商店确认已准备就绪。

我参与过服务器压力测试，还需要重新下载吗？

需要。正式版与服务器压力测试版不同。请在平台商店重新下载《失落星船：马拉松》。

我可以在哪些平台游玩《失落星船：马拉松》？

你可以通过Steam（PC）、PlayStation 5和Xbox Series X|S游玩，支持跨平台游戏与跨平台保存。主机玩家需要有效的在线会员订阅才能游玩。

正式版与服务器压力测试版相比有哪些不同？

正式版包含完整进度、全新疾行者外壳、前哨等内容：

  • 完整的阵营与合约进度
    • 全部六大阵营，包括关口基因
  • 全部六款首发疾行者外壳，包括盗贼
  • 三个区域，包括前哨
    • 前哨将在正式发布第二天解锁，给予社区足够时间筹集装备，并在疾行者等级12时解锁该区域。
  • 28种武器，以及模组、植入物、核心等，助你打造理想配置。
  • 可获得的装饰、成就，以及通过百科呈现的剧情。

正式版还包含根据服务器压力测试反馈做出的修复与优化，包括对外围区域（初级）PVP频率的调整，更多改动即将推出。点击此处，了解更多关于我们在服务器压力测试期间收到的反馈。

前哨、低温档案室和排名模式何时上线？

  • 前哨将在游戏发布次日开放，需达到疾行者等级12级。这为你在全力挑战更为险恶的区域之前，预留了一点整备时间。
  • 排名模式将于3月下半月上线。
  • 低温档案室将于3月下半月，在您解锁前往轨道的通路后开放。

我参与过服务器压力测试。奖励何时发放？会过期吗？

服务器压力测试装饰奖励与欢迎储藏箱将在上线首日陆续发放。我们需要一些时间才能将奖励发放给所有人，所以如果你登录后没有在收件箱中看到奖励，请耐心等待——奖励应该很快就会到账。

如果你获得了服务器压力测试奖励，必须在第1赛季结束前登录游戏才能领取。若你在第1赛季期间未登录游戏，将无法领取奖励。

需要回顾你在压力测试中获得的欢迎储藏箱战利品等级吗？来这边。

我在服务器压力测试期间通过观看直播获得的Twitch掉落何时到账？

如果你在Twitch上观看了《失落星船：马拉松》的直播并获得了Twitch掉落，该奖励将发放到你的Bungie.net个人资料，并在3月6日正式上线时在游戏内发放。Twitch赞助包可能需要48小时才能送达你的游戏内邮箱，请务必在4月1日之前进行兑换。

游戏发布时还会有新的Twitch掉落吗？如何获得？

有的！为获得Twitch掉落，请确保完成以下步骤：

  • 将你的Bungie.net账号与你的Twitch账号绑定
  • 在游戏首发窗口期间，前往Twitch分类观看已开启掉落的《失落星船：马拉松》直播
  • 在Twitch物品栏领取每一项奖励，然后登录《失落星船：马拉松》查收

上线周末期间共有6款奖励可领取。请于北京时间3月6日凌晨2点至3月10日凌晨2点观看直播，即可获取：

  • 武器贴纸：乖猫模式（订阅或赠送订阅给游玩《失落星船：马拉松》的创作者）
  • Twitch赞助包，即游戏内标准战利品（观看1小时）
  • 档案徽标：粉色涂鸦Bingus（观看1小时）
  • 档案背景：粉色涂鸦Bingus（观看2小时）
  • 武器贴纸：粉色涂鸦Bingus（观看3小时）
  • 武器皮肤（风格）：白紫配色——弹道狙击步枪（观看4小时）

你还将有机会在3月15日举办的天仓五杯期间，赢取新一批Twitch掉落。届时创作者团队将同台竞技，争夺天仓五最强疾行者桂冠。更多详情，敬请期待！

如何领取各平台专属武器挂件？有哪些挂件？

PlayStation、Steam、Xbox玩家均可获得对应平台专属武器挂件。

Steam玩家上线登录后，将自动获得铁撬武器挂件。

PlayStation玩家可获得追求自由、战斗吉他、怨灵武器挂件。请于3月6日《失落星船：马拉松》发布后，前往PlayStation®Store领取你的奖励。

Xbox玩家上线登录后，将自动获得翡翠接合与翡翠锁扣武器挂件。

游戏发布当天会出现排队登录的情况吗？如何查看服务器状态？

发布当天会非常繁忙！可能出现短暂排队或更新。如需进行维护，我们将通过BungieServerStatus的Twitter与Bluesky账号同步服务器状态。同时，我们也会在官方Discord社区发布更新。

赛季是如何运作的？后续内容在哪里查看？

请前往我们的《失落星船：马拉松》赛季介绍专题文章，获取完整解读，其中还包含第2赛季的前瞻爆料。

如何获取帮助或反馈问题？

  • 访问《失落星船：马拉松》帮助中心，可查询疑难排解与已知问题。奖励发放异常、账号链接错误、游戏崩溃等问题，均可从此处开始解决。
  • 你也可以加入《失落星船：马拉松》官方Discord，并关注MarathonDevTeam的Twitter账号，获取通知或寻求帮助。
