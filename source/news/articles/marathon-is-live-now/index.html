Marathon is Live Now

Your first death awaits

Marathon Dev Team March 5, 2026
community267 words1 min read

Today is the day — we’re pressing the big “GO LIVE” button and putting Marathon fully in the hands of our community.

Marathon wouldn’t be the game it is today without the support and feedback of players all the way from alpha to Server Slam. Today marks the start of the next phase of our journey together, with even more passionate players joining the fold. Together we'll continue shaping Marathon into the best version it can be — and the team is ready to cook.

  • If you want to learn more about our plans for seasons and what’s coming in Season 1 and beyond, head to our Seasons in Marathon blog
  • If you have questions about launch, including how to claim Server Slam rewards, Twitch Drops for launch, and what’s new since the Slam, head to our FAQ 
  • And if you want to just get f*(&$# hyped, pop on the launch cinematic as you finish your download and prepare for Tau Ceti

Marathon is available now on Steam, PlayStation 5, and Xbox Series X|S with cross-play and cross-save.

You can keep up with the latest news by following us on Twitter, TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube. You can also follow the official Dev Team account on Twitter and Bluesky, or join the official community Discord to chat with the dev team and find a crew who’s just as passionate about drinkable food as you are!

And with that, the lost colony of Tau Ceti awaits your arrival. Good luck, careful who you trust, and remember — death is the first step.

就是今天——我们按下巨大的“上线”按钮，将 Marathon 完全交到社区手中。

没有玩家们从 alpha 测试到服务器压测一直以来的支持和反馈，Marathon 就不会是今天的样子。今天标志着我们共同旅程下一阶段的开始，更多充满热情的玩家将加入进来。我们将一起继续将 Marathon 塑造成它最好的形态——团队已经准备好大展身手了。

  • 如果您想了解更多关于我们的赛季计划以及第一赛季及后续内容，请头盔我们的Marathon赛季博客
  • 如果您对发布有任何疑问，包括如何领取服务器压测奖励、发布的Twitch掉宝以及压测之后的更新内容，请头盔至我们的FAQ
  • 如果您只是想热血沸腾一下，可以在下载完成并为鲸鱼座τ星做好准备时，播放发布动画

Marathon现已在Steam、PlayStation 5和XBOX Series X|S上架，支持跨平台游玩和跨平台存档。

您可以通过关注我们的Twitter、TikTok、Instagram和YouTube来了解最新消息。您也可以关注官方的开发团队Twitter账号和Bluesky，或加入官方社区Discord与开发团队聊天，并找到一群和您一样对可饮用食物充满热情的伙伴！

至此，鲸鱼座τ星的失落殖民地在等待您的到来。祝你好运，小心你信任的人，记住——死亡是第一步。
