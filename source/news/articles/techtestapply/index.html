Apply Now: Marathon Closed Technical Test

Since June, we’ve run several closed playtests to gather feedback on Marathon. We’re now opening applications for more players to join us for a Closed Technical Test from October 22 to October 28 . This is a limited, ...

Marathon Dev Team October 6, 2025
community604 words3 min read

Since June, we’ve run several closed playtests to gather feedback on Marathon. We’re now opening applications for more players to join us for a Closed Technical Test from October 22 to October 28. This is a limited, invite-only playtest for players in North America and Europe on PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X|S, and Steam. As with recent closed playtests, this test is under NDA.

CLOSED TECHNICAL TEST APPLICATION

Fill out this form between now and October 16 at 4 PM (PT) to submit your application. If you previously applied for public Alpha from April, the form may automatically submit; no further action will be needed if this happens.

For players on PC, we’ll also be enabling applications via Steam on October 13 at 10 AM (PT), and they’ll remain open until October 26 at 4 PM (PT).

This is an important checkpoint for us as we test our improvements since Alpha, including three maps, five runner shells, prox chat, re-tuned combat pacing, solo queue, deeper environmental storytelling, and more. That said, the Technical Test build is a work in progress and will only include a portion of what’s planned for Marathon’s full release, focused on the early player experience.

We’ll be sharing a public update on Marathon's development in the coming months after the Closed Technical Test!

 

CLOSED TECHNICAL TEST SIGNUP FAQ

  • How long will the Closed Technical Test run?
    • The Closed Technical Test will run from October 22 at 10AM PT, to October 28 at 10AM PT.
    • Participation in previous tests doesn’t guarantee participation in this or future tests.
  • How do I know if I’ve been invited?
    • You will receive an official email from Bungie.
    • Closed Technical Test invites will be sent in waves throughout the test.
  • If I’ve played in the Alpha or other NDA’d playtest, am I automatically invited to the Closed Technical Test?
    • No, you’ll need to apply and be invited to the Closed Technical Test to participate.
  • Am I allowed to stream, record, or share my playtest experience?
    • This test is strictly under an NDA, and as such you will not be able to stream or share your playtest experience publicly.
    • Any feedback or recordings will need to be shared only in the NDA’d section of the Marathon Official Discord.
  • Can I share my invite with someone else (friends/family)?
    • Your invite is specific to you, the Bungie.net account you signed up with, and the Discord account you provided during the sign-up process. You will not be able to share it.
  • How do I get access to the NDA Discord?
    • Join the official Discord at discord.gg/marathonthegame. We will add the Discord account name you provided during sign-up to our NDA’d section. Access for the NDA’d Discord channels can take up to 24 hours to update.
    • For those joining after the test has begun, we’ll be conducting role grants daily in the evening for the duration of the test.
    • For Steam users, you’ll need to take the survey you receive in-game to provide your Discord and Bungie.net account names.
  • How do I report bugs or provide feedback?
    • For bug reporting, you will need a Bungie.net account to access the Marathon Help Center Forums.
    • For feedback, we will be sending out surveys to your email. Additionally, you can provide feedback in our NDA section in Discord.
    • Access for both the forums and Discord can take up to 24 hours to update.
  • What regions are allowed in?
    • North America
    • Europe
  • What are the minimum system requirements?
    • Console:
      • PlayStation 5
      • Xbox Series S
    • PC:
      • Processor: Intel Core i5-6600
      • GPU: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1050 Ti
      • RAM: 8GB RAM
  • Learn More: https://help.marathonthegame.com/

自六月以来，我们进行了多次封闭试玩以收集关于Marathon的反馈。现在，我们正在开放申请，邀请更多玩家参与从10月22日至10月28日的封闭技术测试。这是一次面向北美和欧洲地区PlayStation 5、XBOX Series X|S和Steam玩家的限量邀请制试玩。与近期的封闭试玩一样，本次测试受保密协议约束。

封闭技术测试申请

请在即日起至太平洋时间10月16日下午4点之间填写此表格以提交申请。如果您此前曾报名参与四月的公开Alpha测试，表格可能会自动提交；若出现此情况，则无需进一步操作。

对于PC玩家，我们还将于太平洋时间10月13日上午10点在Steam上开放申请，申请将持续到太平洋时间10月26日下午4点。

这是我们测试自Alpha以来改进的重要节点，包括三张地图、五个疾行者外壳、近距离语音聊天、重新调整的战斗节奏、单排匹配、更深入的环境叙事等。不过，技术测试版本仍在开发中，仅包含为Marathon完整发布所规划内容的一部分，侧重于早期玩家体验。

我们将在封闭技术测试结束后的几个月内分享关于Marathon开发的公开更新！

 

封闭技术测试报名常见问题

  • 封闭技术测试持续多长时间？
    • 封闭技术测试将于太平洋时间10月22日上午10点至10月28日上午10点进行。
    • 参与以往测试不保证能参与本次或未来的测试。
  • 如何知道是否收到邀请？
    • 您将收到来自Bungie的官方邮件。
    • 封闭技术测试的邀请将在测试期间分批发送。
  • 如果我参加过Alpha测试或其他受保密协议（NDA）的试玩，是否会自动获得封闭技术测试的邀请？
    • 不，您需要申请并被邀请才能参加封闭技术测试。
  • 我是否可以直播、录制或分享我的试玩体验？
    • 本次测试严格受保密协议（NDA）约束，因此您不得公开直播或分享您的试玩体验。
    • 任何反馈或录像只能分享在Marathon官方Discord的保密协议（NDA）指定板块中。
  • 我可以将邀请分享给其他人（朋友/家人）吗？
    • 您的邀请与您本人、注册时使用的Bungie.net账号以及注册过程中提供的Discord账号绑定，无法分享。
  • 如何访问保密Discord？
    • 加入官方Discord：discord.gg/Marathonthegame。我们将把您在注册时提供的Discord账号添加到保密专区。保密Discord频道的访问权限可能需要最多24小时才能更新。
    • 在测试开始后加入的玩家，我们将在测试期间每晚进行角色授权。
    • 对于Steam用户，您需要填写在游戏中收到的问卷，以提供您的Discord和Bungie.net账号名称。
  • 如何报告错误或提供反馈？
    • 要报告错误，您需要一个Bungie.net帐户才能访问Marathon帮助中心论坛。
    • 对于反馈，我们将通过电子邮件向您发送调查问卷。此外，您还可以在Discord的NDA版块中提供反馈。
    • 论坛和Discord的访问权限可能需要最多24小时才能更新。
  • 哪些地区可以参加？
    • 北美
    • 欧洲
  • 最低系统要求是什么？
    • 主机：
      • PlayStation 5
      • Xbox Series S
    • PC：
      • 处理器：Intel Core i5-6600
      • GPU：NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1050 Ti
      • 内存：8GB RAM
  • 了解更多： https://help.Marathonthegame.com/
