Marathon Key Dates

Mid-season begins July 21 and includes Vault Breaker, an experimental PVE mode on Cryo Archive.

Marathon Dev Team June 23, 2026
community367 words2 min read

As we move deeper into the new season, we want to share some important dates coming up for Marathon. 

Mid-Season Begins July 21

First up, mid-season will start on July 21 and it will include the following updates.

Introducing Vault Breaker

Marathon's experimental PVE mode: Vault Breaker. Explore Cryo Archive as a crew, duo, or a solo player, taking on a progressively challenging series of vaults. Grow stronger across multiple matches with progression that’s unique to Vault Breaker, and push towards the final vault and the mysterious entity within. 

Vault Breaker requires a special Sponsored Kit to queue up, and any gear or items you find stay behind when you exfil. The exception to this is Vault Data. This new currency is found within vaults, and when extracted, can be exchanged for upgrades to your Vault Breaker Sponsored Kits as well as gear that can be used in other modes. This let’s you experience Cryo Archive without flooding the economy with low-risk, high-power Cryo loot.

Cradle Evolution System

Mid-season also brings the first iteration of the Cradle Evolution system. Upon maxing out your Cradle, you’ll now be able to reset back to zero to earn one additional maximum Energy point and unlock unique cosmetics, including multiple Runner shell styles. These cosmetics will be available later in the season, but if you unlock them ahead of time, you’ll automatically receive them once they’re available. With this change, Cradle progression speeds will also be increased. 

More to come!

On top of Vault Breaker and the Cradle Evolution system, we have other various other quality-of-life, tuning, and other changes, including the addition of player profile stats. We'll share more details when we get closer to release.

Season 3 Begins September 22

Looking further ahead, Season 3 will begin on September 22 and brings big changes to the early game experience, including a revamp of Perimeter to add major new areas, encounters, and gameplay. Season 3 also introduces a new Runner shell, new weapons and equipment, and lots of other content and surprises.

We’ll share more details about mid-season, including a full rundown on Vault Breakers, and our Season 3 plans as we get closer to their live dates.

在新赛季逐步展开之际，我们想与大家分享《失落星船：马拉松》接下来的一些重要日程。 

赛季中期更新将于7月22日开启

第一项重要安排是7月22日开启的赛季中期更新，届时将推出以下更新内容。

全新模式“破库者”

《失落星船：马拉松》的实验性PVE模式：破库者你可以组成完整团队、双人搭档，或独自深入低温档案室，攻克层层升级的保险库挑战。通过破库者专属进度系统，在多场对局中不断强化自身，并朝着终极保险库与其中的神秘存在迈进。 

参与破库者需要装备专属赞助包加入队列，而在疾行中获得的武器、装备和道具均无法携带撤离。不过，保险库数据不受此限制。这种全新货币可在保险库中获得，成功携带撤离后可用于升级你的破库者赞助包，也可兑换适用于其他模式的装备。借助这一设计，我们既能让玩家探索低温档案室，又能避免低风险获取高价值战利品的行为对游戏经济系统造成冲击。

摇篮进化系统

赛季中期还将带来摇篮进化系统的首个迭代版本。在摇篮升至满级后，你可以选择将其重置归零，换取额外的1点最大能量上限，并解锁独特装饰，其中包含多种疾行者外壳风格。这些装饰将在赛季后期上线，若你已提前完成解锁，即可在上线后自动获得。这一调整也会同步提高摇篮的进度提升效率。 

更多消息，敬请期待！

除了破库者和摇篮进化系统外，我们还将带来多项体验优化、调整，并新增玩家档案数据统计。我们将在临近上线时分享更多细节。

第3赛季将于9月23日开启

展望未来，我们将于9月23日开启第3赛季，并对前期游戏体验进行重大调整，其中包括全面重制外围区域，加入全新的关键区域、遭遇战与玩法内容。此外，第3赛季还会推出全新疾行者外壳、武器与装备，以及丰富的未公开内容与惊喜。

我们将在临近上线阶段分享更多关于赛季中期更新的细节，包括破库者的完整介绍以及第3赛季的整体规划。
