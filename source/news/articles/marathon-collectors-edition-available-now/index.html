Marathon Collector’s Edition – Available Now

Featuring a 1/6-scale Thief Runner Shell statue, collectible miniature WEAVEworm, and more.

Marathon Dev Team January 20, 2026
community227 words1 min read

Designed in partnership with PureArts, the Marathon Collector’s Edition features a premium unboxing experience inspired by Sekiguchi Genetics’ shell-weaving process. Unbox to reveal a 1/6-scale Thief Runner Shell statue, collectible miniature WEAVEworm, embroidered patch, postcards, digital rewards, and more.

Having ambushed a rival Runner that now lies in the waters beneath their feet, Thief surveils the surroundings for their next target—and their next payout.

 

“Dire Marsh is harsh on those who lack the confidence to own its danger. A Runner can’t trespass down in the muck without expecting to face the violent consequences of their ambitions. After all, paydays are never free, and survival has many costs.

But even against all odds, across the most hostile environments, a Thief favors grace and style as hallmarks of their lethality.

To Runners kitted out in bio-cybernetic Thief shells, elegance is a weapon—used to distract, designed to leave an impression. They want you to remember who beat you—to the take, to the exfil, in a fight.

For a Thief, style is substance.

And for their victims and the world, it’s a calling card and a reminder to never underestimate the confidence required in showing up to a gunfight set to impress.”

Marathon Collector’s Edition is now available for purchase on the Bungie Store. Marathon is available for pre-order on Steam, PlayStation 5, and Xbox Series X|S.

《失落星船：马拉松》典藏版由PureArts合作设计，以关口基因的外壳编织工艺为灵感，带来尊贵的开箱体验。打开包装，即可获得1/6比例的盗贼疾行者外壳雕像、织虫迷你收藏模型、刺绣标识、明信片、数字奖励等。

盗贼伏击了一名敌对疾行者，如今这名疾行者正躺在其脚下的水中。盗贼正在环顾四周，寻找下一个目标——以及下一笔报酬。

 

“对于缺乏自信拥抱危险的人来说，恐怖沼泽异常残酷。踏足沼泽的疾行者必须要料到自己野心所带来的暴力后果。毕竟，天底下没有白白的午餐，生存要付出许多代价。

即使面对重重困难，在最恶劣的环境中，盗贼依然以优雅和风度作为其致命性标志。

对于穿着生物控制盗贼外壳的疾行者来说，优雅是一种武器——用来分散注意力，令人印象深刻。他们想让你记住，无论是抢资源、抢撤离，还是在战斗中，你是谁的手下败将。

对盗贼而言，风格即本质。

对于对手和世界而言，这既是一张名片，也是一个提醒：在枪战中展现自信并给人留下深刻印象，需要极大勇气，不容小觑。”

《失落星船：马拉松》典藏版现可在Bungie商店购买。《失落星船：马拉松》现可在Steam、PlayStation 5和Xbox X/S系列进行预购。
