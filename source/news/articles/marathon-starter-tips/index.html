Marathon Starter Tips

Tips to help you find your footing on the surface!

Marathon Dev Team March 5, 2026
community451 words2 min read

Welcome to Tau Ceti IV. Here are some tips to help you find your footing on the surface! 

FIRST STEPS 

  1. If you breeze through the tutorial, skip “Perimeter (Beginner)” and go right to “Perimeter” in zone select for more PvP encounters 
  2. Bring plenty of ammo and healing items. You won’t find a lot in-run. 
  3. You’ll unlock cosmetics for free in the Codex as you play. Scan its pages for any that catch your eye and prioritize those objectives! 

 

TRAVERSAL 

  1. Crouching makes heat dissipate faster
  2. Everything makes noise. Moving, looting, activating exfils, opening doors, using items, healing, swapping weapons, and - of course - shooting things. Stay quiet or you'll give away your location. 
  3. The world is littered with threats like tick nests and UESC claymores. The poison plants are especially deadly. 

 

COMBAT 

  1. The Equipment radial (hold 5 / Left D-Pad) lets you use grenades, ammo crates, and other deployables regardless of which one you have currently equipped
  2. Some weapons have Z / Left D-Pad functions like alternate scopes and gun shields
  3. Smoke fully drops UESC aggro

 

SURVIVAL 

  1. Smart Heal (4 / Down D-Pad) uses the most effective item for your situation. Manually use specific items by holding 4 / Down D-Pad and selecting from the radial. 
  2. Depleted Patch Kits and Shield Charges don’t exfil with you, even for credits. Use them liberally and drop them at the end of the run to make space for other loot! 
  3. Containers on the outskirts are more likely to have healing items 

 

EXFIL 

  1. Exfils spawn semi-randomly throughout a match, not at fixed locations on match start 
  2. Exfils take about a minute to warm up after activation. You don’t need to stand in them until the circle appears. 
  3. Guarded Exfils spawn a UESC patrol when activated 

 

AMMO 

  1. Keep an eye out for Ammo Crates, not just raw ammo 
  2. Conserve ammo by knifing low-level enemies 
  3. Containers on the outskirts are more likely to have ammo 

 

FACTIONS & CONTRACTS 

  1. Want more mobility? Extra vault space? Better purchasable weapons? Read through each faction’s upgrade tree to pick which one to level up first. 
  2. Some contracts are zone-specific. 
  3. Hover over contract objectives on the map screen for info on where to complete them 

 

ARMORY & BARTERING 

  1. Some items are bartered for loot, not sold for credits 
  2. Track needed bartering loot (R / R3) to mark drops in-game 
  3. Unstable Materials can always be traded in for ammo, Patch Kits, and Shield Charges 

 

BUILDCRAFTING 

  1. You can freely swap cores, implants, and weapon mods in and out of run 
  2. Inspect weapons out of run (V / Triangle / Y) to bring up a mod workbench 
  3. Only Standard (gray) tier cores can be used by every shell. All others require specific shells to use. 

欢迎来到天仓五IV。以下是一些小贴士，帮你快速上手！ 

第一步 

  1. 如果你轻松完成了教程，可跳过“外围区域（初级）”区域，直接在区域选择界面选择“外围区域”，体验更多PvP战斗。 
  2. 携带充足的弹药和治疗物品。疾行期间这些物品不会很多。 
  3. 在游戏过程中，你会逐渐在百科中免费解锁装饰。浏览页面，找出你感兴趣的内容，并优先完成这些目标！ 

 

移动 

  1. 蹲伏可以更快地散热。
  2. 所有动作都会发出声响。移动、掠夺、激活撤离点、开门、使用物品、治疗、切换武器，当然还有射击。保持安静，否则你会暴露自己的位置。 
  3. 世界遍布各种威胁，例如虱虫巢穴和UESC阔刀地雷。毒草尤其致命。 

 

战斗 

  1. 无论你当前装备如何，你可以通过装备轮盘（按住5/左方向键）来使用手雷、弹药箱和其他部署物。
  2. 一些武器具有Z/左方向键的功能，例如切换瞄准镜和枪盾。
  3. 烟雾可完全解除UESC的敌对状态。

 

生存 

  1. 智能治疗（4/下方向键）会根据你当前的情况使用最有效的物品。按住4/方下向键，然后从轮盘中选择，即可手动使用特定物品。 
  2. 耗尽的修补包和护盾电池也不会随你撤离，即使换成信用点。尽量多加使用，并在疾行结束时将其丢弃，以便腾出空间存放其他战利品！ 
  3. 外围的容器更有可能包含治疗物品。 

 

撤离 

  1. 在整场比赛中撤离点会半随机出现，而不是在比赛开始时固定出现在某个位置。 
  2. 撤离点激活后大约需要一分钟的预热时间。你不需要站在其中直到圆圈出现。 
  3. 激活受到把守的撤离点时，会生成UESC巡逻兵。 

 

弹药 

  1. 留意弹药箱，而不仅仅是散装弹药。 
  2. 用刀攻击低等级敌人可以节省弹药。 
  3. 外围的容器更有可能包含弹药。 

 

阵营与合约 

  1. 想要更高的机动性？额外的保险库空间？购买更好的武器？阅读各阵营的升级树，选择优先升级哪个阵营。 
  2. 部分合约仅限特定区域。 
  3. 将鼠标悬停在地图界面的合约目标上，来查看在哪里完成目标。 

 

军械库与交换 

  1. 部分物品可用于交换战利品，而非出售换取信用点。 
  2. 追踪所需的交换战利品（R/R3），来标记游戏内的掉落物品。 
  3. 不稳定材料始终可用于兑换弹药、修补包和护盾电池。 

 

配装 

  1. 你可以在疾行内外自由切换核心、植入体和武器模组。 
  2. 在疾行外检查武器（V/三角/Y）即可打开模组工作台。 
  3. 只有标准（灰色）级核心才能用于所有外壳。其他核心需要特定的外壳才能使用。
