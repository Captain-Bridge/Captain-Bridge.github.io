Marathon Update 1.1.0.2

The one about the C.A.R.R.I protocol returning.

Marathon Dev Team June 16, 2026
updates848 words4 min read

General

  • ONI is now announcing the return of C.A.R.R.I., the CyberAcme Runner Reinforcement Initiative. This Initiative returns with the following changes and additions:
    • CyberAcme Commendations: A new material that is awarded to Runners who promote C.A.R.R.I. values
      • For Crews: Earn commendations (+4) for each contract objective you assist your crewmate in completing
      • For Solos (Non-Rook): Earn commendations (+6) for each contract objective you complete. Bonus commendations (+5) can be earned if you exfil with other players.
      • For Rooks: Earn commendations (+5) for successful exfils. Bonus commendations (+5) can be earned if you exfil with other players.
      • All commendation rewards will be sent directly to your vault after completing any of the actions listed above
    • C.A.R.R.I. Armory: Exchange commendations for special offers in CyberAcme’s armory.
      • Weapon Showcase: Each week, C.A.R.R.I. will feature a new weapon. An enhanced weapon will be given for free, but runners will have the opportunity to kit up their own and even buy a unique, deluxe variant.
      • Reputation Packs: Can only be bought from the C.A.R.R.I. Armory. Purchasing one will automatically grant reputation for the associated faction.
      • Special Offers: In addition, C.A.R.R.I. will provide an assortment of useful items and equipment. Keep an eye on offers as the season progresses.
    • Fixed an issue where the previously selected zone would reset after each run.
    • Fixed an issue where your crew fill settings did not persist between runs.
    • Fixed an issue where Rooks were able to queue as a Duo.
    • Fixed an issue that prevented Crew members from readying up after swapping from Trios to Duos.
    • Fixed an issue where a Duo may be unable to matchmake if the leader changes the activity to a non-Duos zone.
    • Fixed an issue where a crew error would display if Crew-fill was enabled while on Duos.

Item Economy

  • Added weekly login rewards beneath the "today's sponsors” section in the Armory.
  • Slightly reduced loot quality and increased item pool in Cert containers in Night Marsh.
  • Slightly reduced Prestige drops from pinnacle containers found in Prestige locked rooms.
  • Fixed an issue where combatant reinforcements in Night Marsh were able to roll more loot than intended over the course of the run.
  • Minor rebalancing of Folio and Trunk rewards across item types.
  • Fixed an issue where UESC Sponsored Kits could spawn with Cryo keys.

Cradle

  • Reduced XP value of “Bluenique” (deluxe unique weapons) from 1,500 to 700.
  • Reduces XP value of Superior and Prestige weapon mods:
    • Superior: 2,500 to 1,000
    • Prestige: 7,500 to 3,750

Combat

Weapons

  • Fixed an issue where Folding Stocks stat bonuses were reversed based on their configuration.

Bully SMG

  • Fixed an issue with Bully Twitch sub drop style’s magazine not matching the promotional artwork.

BR33 Volley Rifle

  • Fixed an issue where the Lockout Muzzle Break Prestige mod was not suppressing the weapon audio for the user. (Audio was properly suppressed for other Runners.)

V22 Volt Thrower

  • Fixed an issue where firing the V22 Volt Thrower could be inaudible to crewmates and other Runners.

User Interface and Experience

  • Errors in the Ready Up screen will now more clearly display which crewmate has an error. Hovering over the error will display a tooltip explaining how to resolve it. Crew errors (such as too many/not enough Runners) will display only near the ready button.
  • Fixed an issue where some weapon mod tooltips would flash or not show when comparing with another compatible mod.

Runners

Thief

  • Fixed an issue where Thief could say the wrong voice lines in the Runner select or when damaged in-run while equipped with the Kasha Yokai shell style.

Contracts

  • Fixed an issue where the wrong art was being displayed on the “Story & Rewards” side of certain limited contracts.

CyberAcme

  • Fixed an issue that could prevent some limited Cryo contracts from appearing correctly after weekly reset.

NuCaloric

  • Fixed an issue with Capstone 2 where patch kits and shield charges were not being awarded upon completing standard contracts.
  • Fixed an issue that prevented anti-virus packs from being available in the Armory, even after unlocking them in the Faction upgrades.

Codex

  • Fixed an issue with entry “One man’s treasure” where it would complete without needing to convert a Prestige rarity item in the Cradle.
  • Updated tooltip description for Bungie Reward entries to indicate that rewards are available for purchase after claiming.
  • Fixed an issue where some “Early Bird VIP” entries from Season 1 were not unlocking correctly in Season 2.

Zones

Dire Marsh (Day and Night)

  • Fixed an issue that allowed Runners to loot certain containers in the Bioresearch locked room without the proper key.
  • Fixed an issue where a non-final exfil's timer could be extended beyond the final exfil's timer, resulting in Runners dying to the timer even while standing in an exfil.
  • Fixed an issue causing a few containers to float on top of Greenhouse.

Dire Marsh (Night)

  • Fixed an issue in Upper Complex where a door hack device was hidden behind a cabinet.
  • Fixed an issue where other Runners' flashlights wouldn't appear if they were turned on before entering your sight range.

Dire Marsh (Day)

  • Fixed an issue where the second wave of exfils was failing to spawn.

综合

  • ONI宣布赛博艾克米疾行者强化计划（C.A.R.R.I.）现已回归。此计划本次回归带来了改动和新增内容：
    • 赛博艾克米嘉奖：一种奖励给践行C.A.R.R.I.价值观的疾行者的全新材料
      • 对于团队：辅助队友完成每个合约目标，即可获得嘉奖(+4)
      • 对于单人（非鲁克）：完成每个合约目标，即可获得嘉奖(+6)。与其他玩家一同撤离可获得额外嘉奖(+5)。
      • 对于鲁克：成功撤离可获得嘉奖(+5)。与其他玩家一同撤离可获得额外嘉奖(+5)。
      • 完成上述任何操作后，所有嘉奖奖励将直接发送至你的保险库
    • C.A.R.R.I.军械库：在赛博艾克米军械库中兑换嘉奖，获取特殊优惠。
      • 武器展示：C.A.R.R.I.每周都会推出一种新武器。玩家将免费获得一件强化武器，但疾行者也有机会自行装备武器，甚至可以购买独特的豪华版本。
      • 声望包：只能在C.A.R.R.I.军械库购买。购买一个声望包即可自动提升对应阵营的声望。
      • 特别优惠：此外，C.A.R.R.I.还将提供各种有用物品和装备。随着赛季的进行，请密切关注优惠活动。
    • 修复了每次疾行结束后，之前选择的区域都会重置的问题。
    • 修复了团队补位设置无法在疾行之间保存的问题。
    • 修复了鲁克可以双人队列进行排队的问题。
    • 修复了从三人切换至双人后，队员无法准备就绪的问题。
    • 修复了如果队长将活动区域更改为非双人区域，则双人组可能无法匹配的问题。
    • 修复了在双人模式下启用团队补位时，会显示团队错误的问题。

物品经济

  • 在军械库的“今日赞助商”部分下方添加了每周登录奖励。
  • 小幅降低了夜间沼泽中认证容器的战利品品质，并增加了物品种类。
  • 小幅降低了尊享上锁房间中至臻容器的尊享掉落。
  • 修复了夜间沼泽中战斗人员增援在疾行过程中掉落的战利品数量超出预期的问题。
  • 对不同物品类型的收纳箱和杂物箱奖励进行了微调。
  • 修复了UESC赞助包可能生成低温钥匙的问题。

摇篮

  • 将豪华独特武器的经验值从1500降低至700。
  • 降低了卓越和尊享武器模组的经验值：
    • 卓越：2500至1000
    • 尊享：7500至3750

战斗

武器

  • 修复了折叠枪托的属性加成会根据其配置反转的问题。

恶霸冲锋枪

  • 修复了恶霸Twitch订阅掉落风格弹匣与宣传图不符的问题。

BR33连发步枪

  • 修复了锁定式枪口制退器尊享模组无法抑制使用者的武器音效的问题。（其他疾行者的音频已正确抑制。）

V22伏特放射器

  • 修复了V22伏特放射器射击时，队友和其他疾行者可能听不到声音的问题。

用户界面和体验

  • 准备界面中的错误现可更清晰地显示是哪位队员出现错误。将鼠标悬停在错误上会提示如何解决该错误。团队错误（例如疾行者过多/过少）只会显示在准备按钮附近。
  • 修复了某些武器模组提示在与其他兼容模组进行比较时会闪烁或不显示的问题。

疾行者

盗贼

  • 修复了在疾行者选择界面或装备火之车妖怪外壳风格时，盗贼在疾行中受到伤害可能会说出错误语音的问题。

合约

  • 修复了某些限定合约的“剧情和奖励”页面显示错误图片的问题。

赛博艾克米

  • 修复了每周重置后部分限定低温合约可能无法正确显示的问题。

纽卡洛里

  • 修复了巅峰2中完成标准合约后无法获得修补包和护盾电池的问题。
  • 修复了即使在阵营升级中解锁了杀毒包，也无法在军械库中找到的问题。

百科

  • 修复了“彼之蜜糖”条目无需在摇篮中转换尊享稀有度物品即可完成的问题。
  • 更新了Bungie奖励条目提示描述，说明奖励领取后即可购买。
  • 修复了第1赛季的部分“早鸟VIP”条目在第2赛季无法正确解锁的问题。

区域

恐怖沼泽（白天和夜间）

  • 修复了疾行者无需正确钥匙即可获取生物研究所上锁房间某些容器的问题。
  • 修复了非最终撤离点的计时器可能比最终撤离点的计时器更长，疾行者即使站在撤离点内，也会因计时器超时而死亡的问题。
  • 修复了温室上方部分容器漂浮的问题。

恐怖沼泽（夜间）

  • 修复了上层设施中门禁骇入装置隐藏在橱柜后面的问题。
  • 修复了其他疾行者在进入你的视野范围之前打开手电筒，而手电筒不会显示的问题。

恐怖沼泽（白天）

  • 修复了第二波撤离点无法生成的问题。
