Marathon Community Comms: Where To Find Us

We’re bringing the latest and greatest, straight to you

Marathon Community Team March 26, 2026
community958 words4 min read

We have a soft spot for a good blog. Over the years at Bungie, they’ve been a place to gather news, share stories, and point you toward what's coming next. With Marathon, you'll see us talking in more places than ever – so we wanted to take a moment to let you know where you can find us.

Think of this blog as your one-stop shop for all things Marathon comms. If you want quick service updates, deeper context around changes, or a place to talk with other Runners, you should always know where to go and what to expect when you get there.

When we sat down to map out how we talk about Marathon with our community, three values kept rising to the top. They shape how often you hear from us, what we say, and where you will find it, whether we are walking through a small hotfix or a major update.

Think of them as the ground rules for how we show up in your feeds.

  • Low latency: When something happens, we talk about it quickly. If an issue pops up in the middle of your evening, you shouldn’t have to wait until next week to know what’s going on.
  • High accuracy: We keep updates smaller and more focused so the information you see is clear, current, and easy to trust.
  • Close to you: We share news in the places you already check. That means Discord, in-game notices, and social channels you use every day.

Everything that follows in this post ties back to these three values, and how they show up across each of our channels.

Here’s a quick rundown on how we plan to share info on other platforms.

Service and Status

If something might change your night, start on Discord. The #marathon-news and #server-status channels are where we post first. You can also find the latest server updates from BungieServerStatus on Twitter and Bluesky. (And for major events, you’ll also see an in-game message.)

Maintenance gets the full set, both on Discord and BungieServerStatus. Heads up the day before, a reminder an hour out, a start post with the expected end time, then a done post that includes a link to any relevant patch notes.

Depth and Context

When you want the “why” behind the “what,” make sure to point yourself here to Bungie.net for any lengthier blog updates. These will be on less of a specific cadence, so keep your eyes on our MarathonTheGame social media channels to ensure you don’t miss them when they arrive.

Discord is the Hub

We are standing up clear, focused channels so you can choose your own signal to receive.

  • #marathon-news: Where you’ll find all of our latest updates as they happen – and thankfully, it’s also a Discord Announcements channel so you can import it directly to your own server.
  • #server-status: Any updates that are relevant to your gameplay experience will land here.
  • #dev-updates: Patch notes, feature updates, and known issue lists will all land in here. Follow here to make sure you get the nitty gritty details.
  • #marathon-community-art-chat: A great place to drop by to find out some of the latest and greatest creators and artists in the Marathon community.

In-Game Notices

When something is critical, we will use in-game messages to reach everyone. That includes incidents, maintenance windows, updates, system changes, and known severe issue disclaimers.

We may also use this space to raise awareness for community events and special opportunities from time to time, but won’t be delivering every single piece of info here to avoid overwhelming your in-game mailboxes.

Social Media

We know a great many of you spend your time out in the wild world of social media. It just so happens, we do too.

  • On @MarathonTheGame, you’ll see the big picture. That’s where we will share trailers, major announcements, events, and community highlights, the kinds of updates you’ll want to share with your crew.
  • On @MarathonDevTeam, we will go deeper into the details. Expect links to patch notes, system updates, developer insights, and more of the nuts and bolts from the team building the game.

We’ll keep our posts clear and direct. If you have thoughts, questions, or longer feedback, Reddit, Steam, and Discord will be great places for us to dig in with you. We have accounts across the board, so keep an eye out for us!

Behind the Curtain

Together with our colleagues here at Bungie, we run a bevy of cross-functional syncs with teams across Design, Engineering, Support, Live Ops, Comms, and more. These teams will also work with us to make sure that we’re erring on the side of accuracy - and, from time to time, you might see a post directly from one of them as well.

All in all though, our goal as the Marathon Community Team remains abundantly clear - to make sure that you always have the latest and most accurate information possible.

Where to Follow

For big moments, trailers, and highlights you’ll want to share with your Crew, be sure to follow our official Marathon social channels:

  • Official Discord
  • Twitter
  • YouTube
  • Bluesky
  • TikTok
  • Instagram
  • Twitch

For information and insights from the Marathon Development Team itself, be sure to follow us on your social channels of choice:

  • Twitter
  • Bluesky
  • Reddit
  • Steam Forums
  • Steam Blog

And of course, for live service updates, maintenance timing, and anything that might affect your ability to play, be sure to follow our BungieServerStatus social media channels:

  • Twitter
  • Bluesky

What to do Today

Join the Discord and follow a Discord Announcement channel or two. 

Follow the accounts above. 

If you spot an issue, don’t hesitate to shout it out and let us know. 

We will keep tuning it with you.

我们始终欣赏用心撰写的优质博客。多年来，Bungie的博客一直是汇集资讯、分享故事，并带你了解未来动态的重要平台。围绕《失落星船：马拉松》，我们的沟通平台将比过去更加多元，因此我们想借此机会介绍这些信息发布渠道。

你可以把这篇博客看作获取《失落星船：马拉松》通讯的一站式服务点。无论你是想获取快速的服务更新、深入了解改动背景，还是与其他疾行者交流，都应该明确知道该前往何处、以及能在那里获得哪些信息。

在我们思考与社区探讨与交流《失落星船：马拉松》的方式时，有三项理念被反复强调并成为重中之重。无论是进行小型热修复还是大型更新，这些理念都会影响我们的沟通频率、内容以及发布渠道。

你可以将它们理解为我们在发布动态时所遵循的基本准则。

  • 快速响应：一旦出现任何情况，我们会尽快进行说明。即便你在夜间游戏时发现问题，也无需等上一周才能了解情况。
  • 高度准确：我们会将更新内容控制得更加精简明确，确保信息的清晰度、即时性和可靠性。
  • 触手可及：我们会在你平时关注的平台上发布消息。其中包括Discord、游戏内通知，以及你每天使用的各类社交渠道。

本文接下来的所有内容都将基于这三项理念，并说明它们在不同渠道中的落实方式。

下面简单介绍一下我们将如何在其他平台上发布信息。

服务与状态

如有任何可能影响你当晚游戏的改动，我们会首先发布于Discord。最新消息通常会第一时间发布在#marathon-news和#server-status频道。你还可以在Twitter和Bluesky的BungieServerStatus账号查看最新的服务器更新。（如遇重大活动，会通过游戏内消息同步发布。）

维护相关信息会完整发布在Discord和BungieServerStatus上。我们会提前一天发布预告、提前一小时提醒、在维护开始时公布预计结束时间，并在结束后发布完成通知并附上相关更新文档链接。

背景与详解

如果你想了解“改动”背后的“原因”，可以前往Bungie.net查看更详细的博客更新。此类内容不会按固定频率发布，因此请关注《失落星船：马拉松》社交媒体渠道，以免错过更新资讯。

枢纽平台Discord

我们正在打造结构清晰的频道体系，方便你按需选择想要接收的资讯。

  • #marathon-news：所有最新消息都会在此处实时发布，同时它也是Discord的公告频道，你可以直接将其导入到自己的服务器。
  • #server-status：所有与游戏体验相关的更新会集中在此处发布。
  • #dev-updates：更新文档、功能更新和已知问题列表会集中在此处发布。关注该频道，确保自己不会错过任何细节信息。
  • #marathon-community-art-chat：这里也是发现《失落星船：马拉松》社区中优秀新人创作者与艺术家的好去处。

游戏内通知

出现重要情况时，我们会通过游戏内通知向所有玩家传达信息。这其中包括各类事件、维护安排、更新、系统变更，以及已知重大问题的声明。

我们也会不时利用这个渠道宣传社区活动或特殊机会，但不会把所有内容都放在这里，以免淹没你的游戏内邮箱。

社交媒体

我们知道很多玩家经常活跃在社交媒体上。恰好，我们也一样在5G冲浪。

  • 在@MarathonTheGame，你能够掌握整体情况与全局信息。我们将在此处发布预告片、重大公告、活动信息以及社区精选内容，这些都是值得和队友一起分享的内容。
  • @MarathonDevTeam将带你了解更详细、更深入的信息。你可以在这里查看更新文档、系统更新、开发者见解以及更多游戏开发背后的具体信息。

我们的发布内容将尽量清晰明了、直奔重点。如果你想分享自己的想法、问题或详细反馈，Reddit、Steam和Discord都是我们可以共同深入探讨的平台。我们的社交账号遍布多个平台，记得关注我们！

幕后揭秘

在Bungie同事们的协作下，我们会与设计、工程、客户支持、实时运营、沟通等多团队保持跨职能同步。这些团队也会与我们合作，优先保证信息准确性——有时你可能会看到由其中某个团队直接发布的博客内容。

总而言之，《失落星船：马拉松》社区团队的目标始终明确，那就是持续为你提供最及时、最可靠的资讯。
