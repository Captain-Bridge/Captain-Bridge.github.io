Get to Know Marathon’s Bungie Rewards

How to secure exclusive merchandise for your IRL vault.

Bungie Store Team June 23, 2026
community583 words3 min read

With the launch of Marathon’s second season, players around the world have been exploring the dark corners of Tau Ceti IV, defending themselves against threats seen and unseen, and with some skill and luck, successfully exfiling with loads of great loot.

Alongside the new features and challenges in the game, Season 2 also brings the launch of Bungie Rewards in Marathon. Bungie Rewards is a program where players can complete challenges in game to earn access to purchase exclusive digital and real-world merchandise that can’t be found anywhere else.

Here, we’ll take you through how the Bungie Rewards works, give you a look at what’s currently available in Marathon, and set you up for what’s yet to come.

Getting Started

First things first, check out the Marathon Bungie Rewards website. Here you’ll find the current listings of what’s available for Marathon players, as well as information on pricing, important earn-by and purchase-by dates, and the in-game challenges you need to complete to be eligible to earn or purchase Rewards items.

To claim any Rewards unlocked through gameplay, players need to meet the following requirements: 

  • Have your Marathon account linked with your Bungie.net profile.
  • Sign into Marathon.BungieStore.com (or Marathon.EU.Bungiestore.com for players in EU and UK) with the platform account that you play Marathon on. 

If you’re logged into Bungie.net with your Bungie.net profile (and have your Marathon account linked), you’ll be able to automatically see which items you are eligible for.

Shipping

Marathon Bungie Rewards are available to residents of the U.S., Canada, Australia, Japan, the E.U., and U.K (some restrictions may apply, see product pages for details). For orders to the E.U. and U.K., please visit marathon.eu.bungiestore.com. For more details on which countries we ship to (as well as additional information on delivery, shipping rates, and more) please see our Shipping Info page.

Runner Rewards

We recently added two more rewards to our Season 2 Bungie Rewards offerings for Marathon: the Compiler Effigy Pin and the Pinnacle Runner Pin. All four Rewards must be earned by March 31, 2027 at 9:59AM PT and purchased by April 30, 2027 at 11:59PM PT, after which they will no longer be available.

Here’s the round-up of the Bungie Rewards available in Marathon Season 2:

Felini Plush

  • Successfully exfil as all available Runner shells (Assassin, Destroyer, Recon, Sentinel, Thief, Triage, and Vandal) and complete the Renaissance Runner Codex Challenge in Marathon by March 31, 2027 at 9:59AM PT. Not available for shipping to Japan.

Notorious T-Shirt

  • Reach Season Level 100 and complete the Notorious Company Codex Challenge in Marathon by March 31, 2027 at 9:59AM PT. 

Compiler Effigy Pin (NEW!)

  • Successfully exfil from Cryo Archive and complete the In From The Cold Codex Challenge in Marathon by March 31, 2027 at 9:59AM PT. 

Pinnacle Runner Pin (NEW!)

  • Players who reach Pinnacle Rank III in Ranked and complete the Reaching The Peak Codex Challenge in Marathon by March 31, 2027 at 9:59AM PT. Limited to 500 quantity.

To unlock access to Bungie Rewards offers in Marathon, players must first claim the related Codex Challenge in game.

You can expect more Bungie Rewards in future seasons, including a new set of Rewards for Season 3. We hope you enjoy these rewards and we look forward to sharing what’s coming up next. For more, follow us on socials on Twitter and Instagram, and as always, keep an eye on the Marathon Bungie Rewards website for the latest. Until then, see you on Tau Ceti IV!

随着Marathon第二赛季的推出，世界各地的玩家一直在探索鲸鱼座IV的黑暗角落，抵御看得见和看不见的威胁，并凭借一些技巧和运气，成功带着大量战利品撤离。

除了游戏中的新功能和挑战，第二赛季还带来了Marathon中Bungie奖励的推出。Bungie奖励是一个项目，玩家可以通过完成游戏内的挑战来获得购买独家数字和实体商品的资格，这些商品在其他地方找不到。

在这里，我们将带你了解Bungie奖励的运作方式，展示Marathon中当前可用的内容，并为你介绍即将到来的内容。

开始使用

首先，请查看Marathon Bungie奖励网站。在这里，你可以找到当前面向Marathon玩家的商品列表，以及定价信息、重要的赚取和购买截止日期，以及你需要完成的游戏内挑战，以便获得赚取或购买奖励物品的资格。

要领取通过游戏玩法解锁的任何奖励，玩家需要满足以下要求：

  • 将你的Marathon 账号关联到你的Bungie.net个人资料。
  • 使用你玩Marathon所使用的平台账号登录Marathon.BungieStore.com（对于欧盟和英国的玩家，登录Marathon.EU.Bungiestore.com）。

如果您使用Bungie.net个人资料登录Bungie.net（并且已关联您的Marathon账户），您将能够自动看到您有资格获得哪些物品。

配送信息

Marathon Bungie奖励面向美国、加拿大、澳大利亚、日本、欧盟和英国的居民提供（可能有限制条件，详情请参阅产品页面）。对于发往欧盟和英国的订单，请访问 Marathon.eu.bungiestore.com。有关我们配送至哪些国家/地区的更多详情（以及配送、运费等信息），请参阅我们的 配送信息页面。

跑者奖励

我们最近为Marathon的第二季Bungie奖励增加了两项奖励：编译器雕像徽章和巅峰跑者徽章。所有四项奖励必须在太平洋时间2027年3月31日上午9:59之前获得，并在太平洋时间2027年4月30日晚上11:59之前购买，之后将不再可用。

以下是Marathon第二季可用的Bungie奖励汇总：

Felini毛绒玩具

  • 成功以所有可用的疾行者外壳（刺客、毁灭者、侦察、哨兵、盗贼、救援和暴徒）撤离，并在太平洋时间2027年3月31日上午9:59之前完成Marathon中的复兴疾行者 百科挑战。不支持配送至日本。

恶名昭彰T恤

  • 达到赛季等级100，并在太平洋时间2027年3月31日上午9:59之前完成Marathon中的 恶名团伙 百科挑战。

编译者雕像徽章（新！）

  • 成功从低温档案撤离，并在太平洋时间2027年3月31日上午9:59之前完成Marathon中的 In From The Cold 百科挑战。

巅峰奔跑者徽章（新！）

  • 在太平洋时间2027年3月31日上午9:59之前，在排名模式中达到巅峰等级III并完成Marathon中的 Reaching The Peak 百科挑战的玩家。限量500份。

要解锁Marathon中的Bungie Rewards奖励，玩家必须先领取游戏中的相关百科挑战。

未来的赛季中还会有更多Bungie Rewards，包括第3赛季的一套新奖励。希望您喜欢这些奖励，我们期待分享接下来的内容。更多信息，请在Twitter和Instagram上关注我们的社交，并一如既往地关注Marathon Bungie Rewards网站获取最新信息。再见，我们在Tau Ceti IV上等着你！
