Season 1 Comes To A Close…

…Time to see how you all did.

Marathon Dev Team June 1, 2026
bungie534 words2 min read

It’s been three months. Three months of carving your own path across Tau Ceti IV, scavenging supplies, taking out the opposition, and securing new contracts to grow your abilities to their limits. Within that time, you all did some amazing things.

Here’s a look at how every Runner made your mark this season.

Closing Out This Season

As we reach the end of this season, there’s a lot to look forward to. You’ll be securing your loot rewards for your Runner Level you worked hard to build. As mentioned in our Season 2 article, everyone will earn Sponsored Kits based on your Runner Level at the end of this season, unlockable in the Codex once Season 2 starts.

Reach Runner Level 10: Unlock Enhanced CyberAcme Sponsored Kit

  • Enhanced Rarity D54 Battle Pistol
  • Light Ammo
  • 3 Smoke Grenades
  • 2 Random Enhanced Rarity Runner Cores
  • 1 Random Enhanced Rarity Head Implant
  • 1 Random Enhanced Rarity Torso Implant
  • 1 Random Enhanced Rarity Leg Implant
  • 1 Enhanced Rarity Shield Implant
  • 1 Enhanced Rarity Base Backpack
  • 1 Stack of Enhanced Shield Charges
  • 1 Stack of Enhanced Patch Kits
  • 1 Deluxe Shield Charge
  • 1 Deluxe Patch Kit

Reach Runner Level 25: Unlock Enhanced CyberAcme Sponsored Kit

  • You’ll earn another copy of the above Enhanced CyberAcme Sponsored Kit if you achieved Runner Level 25.

Reach Runner Level 50: Deluxe CyberAcme Sponsored Kit

  • Deluxe Rarity Twin Tap
  • Heavy Ammo
  • 3 Smoke Grenades
  • 2 Random Deluxe Rarity Runner Cores
  • 1 Random Deluxe Rarity Head Implant
  • 1 Random Deluxe Rarity Torso Implant
  • 1 Random Deluxe Rarity Leg Implant
  • 1 Deluxe Rarity Shield Implant
  • 1 Deluxe Rarity Base Backpack
  • 2 Stacks of Enhanced Shield Charges
  • 2 Stacks of Enhanced Patch Kits
  • 2 Deluxe Shield Charges
  • 2 Deluxe Patch Kits

Reach Runner Level 75: Superior CyberAcme Sponsored Kit

  • Superior Rarity M77
  • Light Ammo
  • 3 Smoke Grenades
  • 2 Random Superior Rarity Runner Cores
  • 1 Random Superior Rarity Head Implant
  • 1 Random Superior Rarity Torso Implant
  • 1 Random Superior Rarity Leg Implant
  • 1 Superior Rarity Shield Implant
  • 1 Superior Rarity Base Backpack
  • 2 Stacks of Enhanced Shield Charges
  • 2 Stacks of Enhanced Patch Kits
  • 6 Deluxe Shield Charges
  • 6 Deluxe Patch Kits
  • 1 Panacea Kit
  • 1 Self Revive Kit

Remember that these kits are additive! The higher the level, the more Sponsored Kits you’ll get.

What’s Next?

Soon, the next chapter will be upon us. Season 2 starts on June 2, and we’re excited to see Runners dive into the newest content. Our dev insights goes into the latest details of what’s coming, including;

  • An updated Dire Marsh (Night) zone
  • New Sentinel Runner shell
  • Updated sandbox changes to weapons, mods, and chips
  • Our new progression system, the Cradle
  • New weapons, updated contracts, and faction progression improvements

Want to try out the new changes with some friends? Curious about what Season 2 has to offer? Join us from June 2 to June 9 for our Open Play Week! You’ll be able to try out the game without any purchase required* for that week, and any progress you make during the Open Play Week will cross over to your account if you purchase a copy of Marathon after.

 

 

*Gameplay requires internet access and paid-for subscription on console (sold separately)

三个月了。三个月以来，你们在鲸鱼座T星IV上开辟自己的道路，搜集补给，消灭敌人，签订新合约，将能力提升到极限。在这段时间里，你们完成了一些了不起的事情。

一起来看看每位Runner在本赛季是如何留下印记的。

接下来？

很快，下一章即将到来。第二赛季将于6月2日开始，我们很期待看到Runners们深入最新内容。我们的开发者见解介绍了即将到来的最新细节，包括；

  • 一个更新的恐怖沼泽（夜间）区域

  • 我们全新的进度系统，摇篮
  • 新武器、更新后的契约，以及阵营进度优化
