Marathon’s Server Slam Begins February 26

We’re opening up the doors and letting everyone in from February 26 – March 2.

Marathon Dev Team February 12, 2026
community769 words3 min read

Updated on February 24 to correct an error in the Deluxe Arrival Cache rewards.

It's time to begin your journey across the stars.

Marathon’s Server Slam, our open preview weekend*, runs from February 26 at 10 AM PT to March 2 at 10 AM PT. Now’s your chance to jump in, put our servers through their paces, and help us get ready for launch on March 5. You’ll be able to play worldwide on Steam, PlayStation 5, and Xbox Series X|S, with cross-play and cross-save enabled.

We’re also excited to be part of Steam Next Fest!

Play the Slam, Earn Rewards for Launch

If you join us starside, we’ve got some extra gear in store to make sure you’re Tau Ceti ready come launch day.

Everyone who completes the introductory mission will unlock a unique emblem and player background that marks you as part of the first wave of Runners.

On top of that, time spent during the Slam will bank loot rewards you’ll receive at launch, based on how far you progress:

  • Complete your first mission: Unlock the Standard Arrival Cache
    • Standard implants (6x)
    • Standard Runner shell cores (4x)
    • Standard weapon chip mods (6x)
    • Weapons: Overrun and Hardline
  • Reach Runner Level 10: Unlock the Enhanced Arrival Cache (Green)
    • Enhanced implants (6x)
    • Enhanced Runner shell cores (2x for each shell, 12x total)
    • Enhanced weapon chip mods (4x)
    • Weapons: Enhanced Magnum and Enhanced Hardline
  • Reach Runner Level 30: Unlock the Deluxe Arrival Cache (Blue)
    • Deluxe and Enhanced implants (3x each, 6x total)
    • Deluxe and Enhanced Runner shell cores (1x each for each shell, 12x total)
    • Deluxe and Enhanced weapon chip mods (2x each, 4x total)
    • Weapons: Deluxe Magnum and Enhanced Volley Rifle
    • Backpack: Enhanced Base Backpack

The emblem, player banner, and loot rewards will be waiting for you when Marathon launches on March 5.

What You’ll Play During the Server Slam

The Server Slam is a slice of Marathon’s gameplay. Enough to get comfortable, not enough to see everything.

You’ll be able to:

  • Infil into two zones:
    • Perimeter, an edge-site expansion on the colony's outskirts
    • Dire Marsh, the colony’s agricultural research hub
  • Take on the early contracts for five factions: CyberAcme, NuCaloric, Traxus, MIDA, and Arachne
    • Progress though the early faction levels and progression trees for each faction
  • Try out five of the six Runner shells that will be available at launch, plus our scavenger experience Rook
  • Play as a crew, solo Runner, form uneasy alliances with proximity chat, and more

 

Launch and Season 1 will bring additional maps, factions, contracts, gear and more, including:

  • Two more zones
    • Outpost, set in the middle of a thriving UESC military installation
    • Cryo Archive, an end-game zone aboard the derelict UESC Marathon (unlocks during Season 1)
  • Thief, the sixth Runner shell
  • Full progression for all six factions, including additional contracts, deeper upgrade trees, and the addition of the sixth faction Sekiguchi Genetics
  • Ranked mode (unlocks during Season 1)
  • Other secrets and surprises...

Marathon releases on March 5

Marathon officially launches on March 5, 2026 on Steam, PlayStation 5, and Xbox Series X|S, with cross-play and cross-save supported at launch.

You can pre-order now to secure additional in-game rewards, including weapon cosmetics, an emblem, and player background for launch, plus a set of Destiny 2 rewards linked to your Bungie.net account.

Pre-order here:

  • Steam
  • PlayStation 5
  • Xbox Series X|S
  • Collector’s Edition

PlayStation Plus Weapon Charms

And we’ve got just one more thing for you.

If you’re playing on PS5 with PlayStation Plus at launch, you’ll get access to a set of exclusive weapon charms inspired by:

  • Helldivers 2 (For Freedom Weapon Charm)
  • Death Stranding 2 (Battle Guitar Weapon Charm)
  • Ghost of Yōtei (Onryō Weapon Charm)

PlayStation Plus members can redeem all three weapon charms at PlayStation ®Store when Marathon releases on March 5.

We'll have weapons charms for Steam and Xbox players as well, which we’ll share more about in the future.

Come on and Jam

Whether you’re here to loot powerful items, hunt players, take down UESC, or just see what Marathon actually feels like to play—we’d love to have you in the Server Slam. Jump in, stress the servers, earn your rewards, and help us get ready for March 5.

*Gameplay at full launch requires internet access and paid-for subscription on console (sold separately).

服务器压力测试中可游玩的内容

服务器压力测试开放了《失落星船：马拉松》的部分玩法。足够让你熟悉游戏，但不足以体验全部内容。

你可以：

  • 渗透两个区域：
    • 外围区域，殖民地边缘的扩张区
    • 恐怖沼泽，殖民地的农业研究中心
  • 接取五大阵营的初期合约：赛博艾克米、纽卡洛里、特莱克斯、米达和阿拉克尼
    • 推进各阵营的前期等级并解锁成长树
  • 试用正式发布版全六款疾行者外壳中的五款，并体验拾荒者鲁克
  • 组队或单人游玩，通过近距离聊天构建临时联盟等多种玩法

 

游戏发布和第一赛季期间将推出更多地图、阵营、合约、装备等内容，包括：

  • 两个新增区域
    • 前哨，坐落于繁忙的UESC军事设施中央
    • 低温档案室，位于废弃UESC马拉松号上的终局区域（第一赛季解锁）
  • 盗贼，第六款疾行者外壳
  • 六大阵营完整进度，包括新增合约、更深入的升级树，以及第六阵营关口基因的加入
  • 排名模式（第一赛季解锁）
  • 其他秘密与惊喜…
