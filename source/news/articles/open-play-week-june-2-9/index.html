Open Play Week: June 2 - June 9

Check out the full game this week, including all of Season 2 and the new experimental mode

Marathon Dev Team June 1, 2026
community1052 words5 min read

Open Play Week Begins June 2 

At the start of Season 2, we’re opening the gates of Marathon so everyone can experience the new content and the many improvements we have made throughout Season 1. This will be a great time to invite your friends to crew up or check the game out for yourself, as everyone will be starting out on a fresh page and learning what’s new together.

During the Open Play Week* from June 2 to June 9, you can experience everything the new season has to offer, including the new Night Marsh zone, a new Runner shell, new gear, and more. Anyone who chooses to purchase will keep all items and progression after the Open Play Week ends. As a reminder, for those who already own Marathon, Season 2 gameplay content will be available for no additional cost.

New Experimental Mode: Sponsored Survival

The start of Season 2 also brings a new experimental mode to Marathon from June 2 to June 9. As mentioned in our recent post, we want to explore more survival experiences for different moods, like if you want to go full sweat or would rather lean back and chill. This new experimental mode lands somewhere in between, and is a more PVP-lite and low risk way to play Marathon than typical runs.

In Sponsored Survival, a single crew will infil onto the new Night Marsh zone. They’ll start as the only players on the map and can explore, loot, and blast their flashlights without fear of repercussion... from PVP enemies anyways. The UESC will still be there in full force, as will some new enemies that lurk in the shadows.

After a certain period of time, backfilled Rooks can enter the map and begin their own scavenging excursions. Perhaps they’ll remain lone wolves, or maybe they’ll team up with each other or the sole crew on the map—everyone's goal is survival, and how you get there is up to you.

In this mode, your one and only ticket off the map is to survive to the final exfil. Each match has an 18-minute run timer, and once it hits zero, the final exfil will spawn. Everyone will have a limited amount of time to book it over and prepare to make their exit. Once there, the decision is yours: Will you negotiate a temporary truce and exfil as one, or turn your weapons on each other in the dead of night?

This asymmetrical mode changes the dynamics of a run, taking the heat off of the lone crew for the first few minutes of the match, while also keeping some of the fun, social unpredictability that Rooks bring to runs. Crews are required to bring a Sponsored Kit loadout to play, which keeps the stakes low for the crew, while also giving Rooks the peace of mind of knowing there’s no fully kitted-out teams on the map.

When you play as the crew, you can use queue fill to find teammates, or disable it to play as a duo or solo. While this mode is running, other Rook queues will be disabled and all Rooks will filter into Sponsored Survival.

Like our previous experimental modes—Duos Queue and Sponsored Queue—Sponsored Survival gives us a chance to test the waters with a different way to play Marathon. Each of these experiments has the potential to inform future experiments, core ways to play, or permanent additions to the game. We’ll also be testing a PVE mode in the latter half of Season 2, with more experiments to come later, such as perhaps a more purely PVP-focused mode.

Shoutout to everyone who’s participated in the experimental queues so far, and please keep your thoughts and feedback coming.

全新实验模式：赞助生存

随着第2赛季开启，《失落星船：马拉松》也将在6月3日至6月10日期间推出全新的实验模式。正如我们近期在博文中提到的，无论你是想全力以赴，还是只想打把休闲局，我们希望能为不同的游玩心境探索多样的生存体验。而这个全新的实验模式，正介于两者之间。相比常规疾行，它可以让你用PVP程度更轻、风险更低的玩法体验《失落星船：马拉松》。

在赞助生存中，一支团队将渗透进入全新的夜间沼泽区域。作为地图上唯一的玩家团队，他们可以自由探索、搜刮物资，甚至肆无忌惮地打开手电筒，而不用去担心遭到袭击……至少不用担心来自敌对玩家的攻击。当然，UESC的部队依旧会全副武装驻守此地，同时还有一些潜伏在黑暗中的新敌人等待着你。

经过一段时间后，补位的鲁克也会进入地图，开始他们各自的搜刮行动。他们也许会选择独自行动，也可能会彼此合作，甚至与地图上的唯一一支团队联手。毕竟，所有人的目标都是活下去。至于通过何种手段达成目的，全由你自己决定。

在这个模式中，你离开地图的唯一方式就是撑到最终撤离。每场比赛的疾行时间限制为18分钟，当计时归零时，最终撤离点就会出现。所有人都必须在有限的时间内迅速赶往撤离区域，并做好离开的准备。到达之后，选择权也交到了你手中：究竟是达成临时休战、一起撤离，还是在暗夜之中，将枪口对准彼此？

这个非对称模式为整场疾行的节奏带来了变化。在比赛开始后的前几分钟，场上唯一的团队不会承受过多的敌对压力。同时也保留了鲁克加入对局所带来的有趣变数。团队必须使用赞助包配装才能进入该模式。这不仅降低了团队的损失风险，也能让鲁克放心游玩，毕竟地图上不会出现武装到牙齿的强力队伍。

当你组队游玩时，可以开启团队补位寻找队友，也可以关闭该功能，以单排或双排游玩。在该模式开放期间，其他鲁克队列都将暂时关闭，所有鲁克都会自动进入赞助生存模式。

和此前的实验模式（双人队列和赞助队列）一样，赞助生存模式让我们有机会测试《失落星船：马拉松》的另一种游玩方式。而每一项实验都有可能影响未来的实验玩法、核心玩法，甚至作为永久模式加入游戏。我们还将在第2赛季后半段测试一个PVE模式，未来也会继续推出更多实验玩法，例如更偏向纯PVP的模式。

也感谢所有热心参与实验队列测试的玩家，请继续和我们分享你的想法与反馈。
