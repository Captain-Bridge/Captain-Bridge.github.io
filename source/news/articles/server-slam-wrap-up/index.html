Thanks for playing in the Server Slam!

Check out fun stats from the Slam and get ready for the real deal on March 5.

Marathon Dev Team March 2, 2026
community378 words2 min read

Thank you to everyone who came out to play in the Server Slam this weekend and helped stress test the servers for launch. We appreciate all your feedback and bug reports, and we’ll share some of our key learnings from the Slam soon.

In the meantime, here’s a look at some community stats from the preview weekend. Drop by our socials if you’d like to see more.

 

Server Slam Rewards on Launch Day

When Marathon launches on March 5, we’ll rollout the rewards you secured during the Server Slam . It will take some time for them to make it to everyone’s in-game  Mailboxes, so if you don’t see them upon logging in hold tight—they should arrive soon.

This includes your cosmetic rewards (Complex Study emblem and Beta.exe background) as well as your Arrival Cache of loot based on your progression during the Server Slam. Head here if you’d like a reminder of what’s in each tier of Arrival Caches.

Note that your Server Slam rewards will expire if you don’t login to claim them before the end of Season 1. So if you played during the Server Slam, but do not log into Marathon before Season 1 ends, you won't receive your rewards.

If you tuned in to Marathon on Twitch and secured Twitch Drops, your rewards will be granted to your Bungie.net profile and delivered in-game* at launch on March 5. The Twitch Sponsored Kit may take up to 48 hours to arrive in your in-game Mailbox and must be redeemed by March 31.

 
Prepare for March 5

Thank you again for participating in the Server Slam. Whether you tried to go for safe runs or were out for blood as Arachne acolytes, we hope your first hours in Marathon were as fun as they were lethal. 

The Server Slam was only a slice of Marathon’s gameplay and progression. Launch will bring the full faction and contract progression, all six launch Runner shells (including Thief), and three zones (including Outpost). The end-game zone, Cryo Archive, on the UESC Marathon ship and Ranked mode are also coming during Season 1, along with other surprises. 

Marathon launches on Steam, PlayStation 5, and Xbox Serries X|S, on March 5. 

 

*Internet connection and online multiplayer subscription may be required. 

感谢这个周末帮助我们在游戏上线前进行服务器压力测试的所有玩家。谢谢你们提供反馈与错误报告，我们很快就会分享来自本次测试的一些重要收获。

与此同时，以下是本次预览周末的一些社区数据统计。欢迎到访我们的社交媒体来了解更多信息。

 

 

 

 

 

 

发布日的服务器压力测试奖励

当《失落星船：马拉松》于3月6日上线后，我们就会开始发放你在服务器压力测试期间获得的奖励。我们需要一些时间才能将奖励发放给所有人，所以如果你登录后没有在收件箱中看到奖励，请耐心等待——奖励应该很快就会到账。

它们包括你的装饰奖励（综合设施徽标和测试版.exe背景），以及基于你在服务器压力测试期间的进度分发的欢迎储藏箱。你可以来这里回顾各个阶级的欢迎储藏箱的具体内容。

请注意，如果你未在第1赛季结束前登录领取，你的服务器压力测试奖励就会过期。因此，如果你参与了服务器压力测试，但未在第1赛季结束前登录《失落星船：马拉松》，你就无法收到奖励。

如果你在Twitch上观看了《失落星船：马拉松》的直播并获得了Twitch掉落，该奖励将发放到你的Bungie.net个人资料，并在3月6日正式上线时在游戏内*发放。Twitch赞助包可能需要48小时才能送达你的游戏内邮箱，请务必在4月1日之前进行兑换。

 
准备迎接3月6日

再次感谢大家参与服务器压力测试。无论你当时是致力于安全撤离，还是以阿拉克尼仆从的身份嗜血厮杀，我们都希望你在《失落星船：马拉松》中度过的头几个小时既充满乐趣，又惊险刺激。 

服务器压力测试仅开放了《失落星船：马拉松》的部分玩法与进度系统。游戏正式上线后将带来完整的阵营与合约进度、全部六款首发疾行者外壳（包括盗贼），以及三个区域（包括前哨）。终局区域，也就是位于UESC马拉松号飞船上的低温档案室，以及排名模式也将在第1赛季内上线，届时还有更多惊喜等你发现。 

《失落星船：马拉松》将于3月6日在Steam、PlayStation 5和Xbox Series X|S平台上推出。 

 

*可能需要互联网连接及在线多人游戏订阅。
