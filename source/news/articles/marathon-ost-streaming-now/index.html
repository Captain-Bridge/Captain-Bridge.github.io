Marathon OST is Streaming Now

Two volumes, 42 tracks, all vibes: available now

Marathon Dev Team March 6, 2026
community489 words2 min read

The Marathon Original Soundtrack, Volume I and Volume II are now available for streaming on all major music platforms.

  • Marathon OST, Vol. I: https://soundtracks.lnk.to/marathonvol1
  • Marathon OST, Vol. 2: https://soundtracks.lnk.to/marathonvol2

Volume I takes the score from Marathon and reimagines it into a linear journey through the world of Tau Ceti, complete with bonus tracks, remixes, and the Son Lux and Poppy collaboration "In Death We’ve Just Begun". Volume II features the full 90+ minutes of in-game score you hear when playing Marathon.

Both volumes of the OST are cleared for use on Twitch—feel free to play them on stream!

When it comes to our partnership with composer Ryan Lott, Audio Director Chase Combs shares...

"Marathon's bold visual direction inspired the Audio team to explore non-traditional approaches to sound — ones that would subvert player expectations and blur the line between music and sound effects. This led to a chance meeting with Ryan during our search for a composer.

In August 2023, Ryan texted me a video shot from his phone of a track playing in his studio. That's when everything clicked. The song was an early sketch of what would become 'Code Race', setting in motion a series of events that shaped not only the score but the sonic identity of Marathon itself."

Want to go behind-the-scenes on the making of Marathon's music? Join Ryan Lott and Chase Combs for a look at the influences, instruments, and inspirations behind the tracks.

It’s been awesome seeing the community resonate so much with Marathon’s music, and we're excited to get the full soundtrack in your hands. Looking forward to hearing all the awesome remixes you put together 🤘

提到我们与作曲家Ryan Lott的合作，音频总监Chase Combs是这么说的…

“《失落星船：马拉松》大胆的视觉风格激励了音频团队去探索非传统的声音设计方向——希望能颠覆玩家预期、模糊音乐与音效的界限。我们在这一方向的指引下寻找作曲家时邂逅了Ryan。

2023年8月，Ryan给我发来一条短信，附上了一段他用手机在工作室拍摄的曲目演奏视频。在那一刻，一切豁然开朗。那首曲目是‘Code Race’的早期雏形，它引发了一连串事件，不仅塑造了整部配乐的面貌，更奠定了《失落星船：马拉松》的声音身份。”

想深入了解《失落星船：马拉松》音乐的幕后制作过程吗？与Ryan Lott和Chase Combs一起探寻这些曲目背后的影响、乐器以及灵感。

很高兴看到社区对《失落星船：马拉松》的音乐产生如此强烈的共鸣，能将完整的原声带正式交到各位手中真是太好了。期待着听到大家创作的各种精彩混音作品 🤘
