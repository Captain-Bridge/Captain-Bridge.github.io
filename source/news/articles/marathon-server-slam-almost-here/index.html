Marathon's Server Slam Is Almost Here

Take your first real steps on Tau Ceti, help us stress test the servers, and earn rewards for launch

Marathon Dev Team February 25, 2026
community1766 words8 min read

It is almost time to light up Tau Ceti.

Marathon’s Server Slam, our open preview weekend*, runs from February 26 at 10 AM PT to March 2 at 10 AM PT. This is your chance to jump in, play Marathon before launch, and help us spin up our global infrastructure at scale for the very first time.

You will be able to play on Steam, PlayStation 5, and Xbox Series X|S, with cross-play and cross-save enabled. 

We are also excited to be part of Steam Next Fest, so you will see Marathon there as well! 

What Makes This A Server Slam

Let us talk about what this weekend is really about.

The Server Slam is a technical stress test. We are turning everything on worldwide, inviting as many of you in as we can, and then watching the systems closely as you do what you do best: log in, crew up, infil, exfil, log out, repeat.

Because of that, you should expect this to feel a little different from a standard launch weekend:

  • You will likely see login queues, especially during peak hours
  • We may need to bring servers down for maintenance on short notice
  • You may run into disconnects, error codes, and bugs

We will be standing by throughout the weekend to address issues, monitor performance, and make adjustments as needed. When something breaks, that is data we can use to make our launch on March 5 all the better.

What You Will Experience During The Slam

The Server Slam is a slice of Marathon. The idea is simple: you get to feel what Marathon actually plays like, with much more waiting for you on March 5.

Across the weekend you will be able to:

  • Infil into two zones on Tau Ceti IV:
    • Perimeter, an edge-site expansion on the colony’s outskirts
    • Dire Marsh, the colony’s agricultural research hub
  • Take on early contracts for five factions: CyberAcme, NuCaloric, Traxus, MIDA, and Arachne
    • Progress through early faction levels and upgrade trees for each of these five factions
  • Try out five of the six Runner shells that will be available at launch, plus Rook, our scavenger experience
  • Play how you like
    • Drop in as a Crew
    • Run solo
    • Form uneasy alliance in real time with proximity chat
    • Or test your foes’ diplomacy by making those alliances fall apart using the aforementioned prox chat

This is a preview weekend, not the full game. When Marathon launches and Season 1 kicks off, you can expect additional zones, factions, contracts, gear, progression depth, Ranked, and some secrets you’ll have to experience yourself.

Launch and Season 1 will bring...

  • The late-game zones Outpost and Cryo Archive
  • Full contract and progression depth for all factions
  • Ranked mode
  • The sixth Runner shell Thief
  • Season 1 unlocks and end-game content
  • And much more!

Launch Day Bonuses For Server Slammers

If you join us starside during the Server Slam, we want you to arrive on launch day looking and feeling ready for all the dangers of Tau Ceti.

Everyone who completes the introductory mission during the weekend will unlock a unique Beta.exe player background and Complex Study emblem that mark you as part of the first wave of Runners. These cosmetic rewards will be exclusive to Server Slam participants.

On top of that, time spent during the Slam will bank loot rewards for launch, based on how far you progress:

Complete your first mission: Unlock the Standard Arrival Cache

  • Standard implants (6x)
  • Standard Runner shell cores (4x)
  • Standard weapon chip mods (6x)
  • Weapons: Overrun and Hardline

Reach Runner Level 10: Unlock the Enhanced Arrival Cache (Green)

  • Enhanced implants (6x)
  • Enhanced Runner shell cores (2x for each shell, 12x total)
  • Enhanced weapon chip mods (4x)
  • Weapons: Enhanced Magnum and Enhanced Hardline

Reach Runner Level 30: Unlock the Deluxe Arrival Cache (Blue)

  • Deluxe and Enhanced implants (3x each, 6x total)
  • Deluxe and Enhanced Runner shell cores (1x each for each shell, 12x total)
  • Deluxe and Enhanced weapon chip mods (2x each, 4x total)
  • Weapons: Deluxe Magnum and Enhanced Volley Rifle
  • Backpack: Enhanced Base Backpack

Cosmetic and loot rewards will be delivered once Marathon launches on March 5. It’ll take a bit of time for us to roll out the rewards to everyone, so if you login and don’t see them in your inbox, hold tight – they should arrive soon!

Watching The Slam: Twitch Drops

If you like to watch as much as you like to run, we have something for you too.

Throughout the Server Slam weekend, you will be able to tune in to anyone streaming Marathon on Twitch and snag a set of cosmetic rewards for launch. Full details on how to secure and claim your rewards will be available on our support pages, but here is the overview:

  • Earn Twitch Drops that will be granted to your Bungie.net profile and delivered in-game* at launch on March 5

There are five Twitch Drop rewards available during the Server Slam weekend, so grab your snacks (NuCal or otherwise) and secure them before launch.

  • Twitch Sponsored Kit, aka Standard in-game loot (Watch for 1 hour)
  • Profile Emblem: SMILEYworm (Watch for 1 hour)
  • Profile Background: WEAVEworm (Watch for 2 hours)
  • Weapon Sticker: GLOWworm (Watch for 3 hours)
  • Weapon Skin (Style): HMG Twitch Engine (Watch for 4 hours)

*Internet connection and online multiplayer subscription may be required.

Join Us For The Slam

Whether you’re here to be a loot goblin, third-party some unsuspecting crews, run real-time combat readiness tests with the UESC, or just to see what Marathon feels like to play – we’d love to have you join us in the Server Slam.

Server Slam FAQ

We know you have questions. Here are answers to some of the big ones.

When is the Server Slam again?

From February 26 at 10 AM PT to March 2 at 10 AM PT.

Where can I play?

The Server Slam will be available on:

    • Steam
    • PlayStation 5
    • Xbox Series X|S

You do not need a PlayStation Plus membership or Xbox Game Pass membership to participate in the Server Slam. Once Marathon launches, an active online subscription service will be required to play on console.

Can I preload for Server Slam?

No, preloading will not be available for the Server Slam. This will help lessen the load on our servers when we first go live.

Preloading will be available for Marathon's launch.

Is cross-play and cross-save supported?

Yes. Cross-play and cross-save will be active for the Server Slam, just like they will be at launch. You will be able to play with friends across platforms and carry your account between them.

Is this the full game?

No. The Server Slam is an open preview weekend that represents a slice of Marathon’s launch content and progression. When Marathon launches and Season 1 kicks off, you can expect additional zones, factions, contracts, gear, progression depth, the Thief Runner shell, Ranked, and some secrets some secrets you’ll have to experience yourself.

Does my Server Slam progression carry over to launch?

Your Server Slam progression will not carry over to launch. However:

    • Your earned launch rewards (emblem, player background, Twitch Drops) will be granted to your account on March 5

    • Your Arrival Caches filled with loot will be delivered to your account on March 5th to help jumpstart your first runs; earn better loot for reaching Runner Level 10 and Runner Level 30

    • The experience you gain learning zones, contracts, and shells will give you a head start on day one

When do I get my rewards?

    • In-game Server Slam rewards (emblem, background, Arrival Caches) will be delivered to your account when Marathon launches on March 5th via the in-game mailbox. It’ll take a bit of time for us to roll out the rewards to everyone, so if you login and don’t see them in your inbox, hold tight – they should arrive soon!
    • Twitch Drops will be granted to your Bungie.net account after you meet the watch requirements and claim them on Twitch, and will appear in-game at launch. They may take up to 48 hours to arrive in your in-game Mailbox and must be redeemed by March 31.

If you run into any issues claiming or receiving rewards, our support team will have a full troubleshooting guide available.

Why is it called a “Server Slam”?

Up until now, we’ve been running monthly playtests to gather feedback about Marathon. These have been essential for improving Marathon alongside the community, but we haven’t had the chance to test our technical infrastructure at scale.

This weekend is all about slamming our servers with as many active players as possible. You bringing your friends and hammering the queues will help us find and fix technical issues before we launch on March 5.

Will there be bugs, queues, or downtime?

Yes. You should expect this to be less stable than launch.

    • Login queues are likely
    • Disconnects and error codes may occur
    • We may bring servers down for emergency maintenance

We will communicate status updates on our official channels and will be actively monitoring telemetry throughout the weekend. If you want to make sure you’re looking at the right places, feel free to join the Marathon Official Discord, follow @MarathonDevTeam on Twitter and Bluesky. You can also follow Bungie Server Status on Twitter or check the Server Status web page for the latest info.

Which regions and languages are supported?

The Server Slam will be available worldwide, with localized text and voice over for all of our supported languages.

什么是服务器压力测试？

我们来聊聊这个周末即将举办的活动的真正目的吧。

服务器压力测试是一次技术性压力测试。我们将在全球范围内全面启动游戏服务，邀请尽可能多的玩家加入游戏。在你们进行那套熟悉的流程（登录、组队、潜入、撤离、退出、再来）时，密切监控系统的表现。

因此，你可能会感觉这与平时的发布周末有些不同：

  • 你可能会在登录时遇到排队的情况，尤其是高峰时段
  • 我们可能需要临时将服务器下线，进行维护
  • 你可能会遇到断线、错误代码和程序错误等情况

我们将在这个周末全天待命，以解决问题、监控性能，并根据需要进行调整。所有出现的问题和得到的数据都能帮助我们继续打磨3月6日的正式上线版本。

服务器压力测试常见问题解答

我们知道你会有疑问。以下是一些重要问题的解答。

服务器压力测试的具体时间是什么时候？

从北京时间2月27日凌晨2点至3月3日凌晨2点。

在哪里可以游玩？

服务器压力测试将在以下平台开放：

    • Steam
    • PlayStation 5
    • Xbox Series X|S

参与服务器压力测试无需PlayStation Plus或Xbox Game Pass会员资格。但在《失落星船：马拉松》正式上线后，在主机上游玩需要有效的在线订阅服务。

服务器压力测试可以预载吗？

不可以，服务器压力测试不支持预载。这将有助于减轻首次开放时的服务器压力。

但在《失落星船：马拉松》正式上线时，我们会提供预载功能。

是否支持跨平台联机和跨平台保存？

支持。和正式上线时一样，跨平台联机和跨平台保存也将在服务器压力测试期间启用。你可以与朋友在不同平台上一起游玩，并在不同平台间继承账号进度。

游戏内容完整吗？

不完整。服务器压力测试是一次公开试玩周末活动，包含《失落星船：马拉松》正式版内容与进度的一部分。在《失落星船：马拉松》正式上线并迎来第一赛季时，你将体验到更多区域、阵营、合约、装备、更深入的进度、盗贼疾行者外壳、排名模式，以及一些必须去亲自体验的秘密。

我在服务器压力测试的进度会继承到正式版吗？

你在服务器压力测试的进度不会继承到正式版。但是：

    • 你所获得的正式版奖励（徽标、玩家背景、Twitch掉落）将在3月6日发放到你的账号

    • 内含丰富战利品的欢迎储藏箱将于3月6日发放至你的账户，帮助你快速开启首次疾行；达到疾行者等级10和30将获得更丰厚的战利品

    • 你在了解区域、合约和外壳过程中积累的经验，也会帮助你在第一天就抢占先机

我什么时候能拿到奖励？

    • 服务器压力测试游戏内奖励（徽标、背景、欢迎储藏箱）将在3月6日《失落星船：马拉松》上线时，通过游戏内邮箱发放到你的账号我们需要一些时间才能将奖励发放给所有人，所以如果你登录后没有在收件箱中看到奖励，请耐心等待——奖励应该很快就会到账！观看压力测试：
    • Twitch掉落将在你满足观看要求并在Twitch上领取后，发放到你的Bungie.net账号，并在正式版发布时出现在游戏中。它们可能需要48小时才能送达你的游戏内邮箱，请务必在4月1日之前进行兑换。

如果你在领取或接收奖励时遇到任何问题，我们的支持团队将为你提供完整的故障排除指南。

为什么叫“服务器压力测试”？

直至目前，我们一直在进行月度试玩测试，收集大家对《失落星船：马拉松》的反馈。这些测试得到的结果有效地帮助我们与社区一起改进《失落星船：马拉松》，但我们还没有机会大规模测试我们的技术基础设施。

这个测试周末的目的就在于，让尽可能多的活跃玩家来压测我们的服务器。大家与朋友一起，将排队队列挤得满满当当，帮助我们在3月6日游戏正式上线前发现并修复技术问题。

会有程序错误、登录排队或服务器关闭的情况吗？

可能会有的。可以想见，这次测试会比正式版的稳定性要差。

    • 可能会出现登录排队的情况
    • 可能会发生断线和错误代码
    • 我们可能会将服务器下线进行紧急维护

我们将在官方渠道进行状态更新，并在整个周末积极监控数据。如果你想确保查看的信息出自正确来源，欢迎加入《失落星船：马拉松》官方Discord，在Twitter和Bluesky上关注@MarathonDevTeam。你也可以在Twitter上关注Bungie服务器状态，或查看服务器状态网页获取最新信息。

支持哪些地区和语言？

服务器压力测试将在全球范围内开放，并同步上线所有支持语言的本地化文本和语音。
