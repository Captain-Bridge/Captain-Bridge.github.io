Marathon Releases March 5, 2026: New Trailer & More

Collector’s Edition, English voice cast, and more revealed. Pre-orders available now.

Marathon Dev Team January 20, 2026
community688 words3 min read

We’re excited to share some big news for Marathon:

Marathon releases on March 5, 2026* on Steam, PlayStation 5, and Xbox Series X|S. Pre-orders are available now.

New gameplay trailer

Our latest gameplay trailer introduces one of the many characters you'll meet on Tau Ceti: Gantry, the MIDA faction agent.

Factions and their agents are one of the ways you’ll experience the story and world of Marathon. As you complete contracts for each of the six factions, you’ll learn more about their identities, motivations, and rivalries as you work together to uncover the mysteries of Tau Ceti’s fate.

Combining world design, gameplay, and discoverable narrative to build a rich, living world to discover is key to Marathon’s DNA.

Completing contracts, finding narrative items, and other achievements will unlock entries in your Codex, which you can view outside of your runs. 

As you collect the colony’s lost audio and text logs, stumble across cryptic graffiti, dig into abandoned colony systems, and aid factions in pursuit of their various goals, you’ll begin a journey into the unknown that is only just beginning.

Announcing the voice cast of Marathon

Today we’re also excited to announce the English-language voice cast of Marathon.

At launch, Marathon features the voices of...

Krizia Bajos, Samantha Béart, Beau Bridgland, Ry Chase, Roger Clark, Darin De Paul, Jennifer English, Dave Fennoy, Nika Futterman, Morla Gorrondona, Reina Guthrie, Donnla Hughes, Keston John, Sohm Kapila, Rich Keeble, Elliot Knight, Erica Lindbeck, Piotr Michael, Brent Mukai, Neil Newbon, Ariana Nicole George, Emily O'Brien, Lee Shorten, Jason Spisak, Ben Starr, JB Tadena, Fred Tatasciore, Craig Lee Thomas, Elias Toufexis, Oliver Vaquer, Scott Whyte, Tracy Wiles, Erin Yvette

Marathon will also have fully-localized dialogue for all of our supported languages, with those voice casts to be introduced at a later date.

Pre-orders are available now

Marathon is available in Standard and Deluxe editions for Steam, PlayStation 5, and Xbox Series X|S, with full cross-play and cross-save**.

Pre-order rewards

You can pre-order Marathon now on Steam, PlayStation 5, and Xbox Series X|S.

Pre-ordering Marathon grants you access to additional rewards, including weapon cosmetics, emblem, and background for Marathon at launch, plus rewards for Destiny 2 linked to your Bungie.net account. Head here for more information.


Marathon Standard Edition

The Marathon Standard Edition includes:

  • Access to all gameplay updates (maps, Runner shells, events, etc.) as the year progresses
  • Pre-order Marathon and also receive the following:
    • ZERO STEP 004 CE Tactical Sidearm Style
    • ZERO STEP RC Weapon Charm
    • ZERO STEP SHIFT Weapon Sticker
    • APOGEE INTERCEPT Background
    • APOGEE INTERCEPT Emblem
  • Plus the following pre-order rewards for Destiny 2 linked to your Bungie.net account:
    • UESC Echo-type Shell Exotic Ghost
    • UESC Rover Exotic Ship
    • UESC Sprinter Exotic Sparrow

Marathon Deluxe Edition

The Marathon Deluxe Edition includes:

  • All Standard Edition content
  • 1x Premium Rewards Pass Voucher
  • 200 SILK Rewards Pass Tokens
  • Weapon Cosmetics:
    • MIDNIGHT DECAY Misriah 2442 Pump Shotgun Style
    • MIDNIGHT DECAY Overrun AR Style
  • Runner Shell Cosmetics:
    • MIDNIGHT DECAY Vandal Shell Style
    • MIDNIGHT DECAY Destroyer Shell Style
    • MIDNIGHT DECAY Assassin Shell Style
    • MIDNIGHT DECAY Thief Shell Style

Marathon Collector’s Edition available now

The Marathon Collector’s Edition features a premium unboxing experience inspired by Sekiguchi Genetics’ shell-weaving process. Unbox to reveal a 1/6-scale Thief Runner Shell statue, collectible miniature WEAVEworm, embroidered patch, postcards, digital rewards, and more.

Head on over to the Bungie Store or check out the video to learn more.

Tau Ceti awaits

We want to take a minute to say thank you to the dedicated community who’s been playtesting Marathon since last April. Your feedback and support have been invaluable, and we can’t wait to see more players on Tau Ceti soon.

We'll share more information about the open preview weekend at a later date.

《失落星船：马拉松》将于2026年3月6日*在Steam、PlayStation 5和Xbox Series X|S推出，现已开放预购。

全新游戏预告片

最新游戏预告片将带你认识你在天仓五里会遇到的众多角色之一：米达阵营特工——甘特里。

阵营及其代理是你体验《失落星船：马拉松》故事与世界的重要窗口。通过为六大阵营完成合约，你将深入理解每个阵营各自的身份认同、行动目标与势力博弈，通过合作共同揭开天仓五命运背后的谜团。

将世界设计、玩法与可探索剧情融合为一体，打造一个丰富鲜活的沉浸世界，正是《失落星船：马拉松》的核心基因。

完成合约、收集剧情道具及达成其他成就后，你将解锁百科条目，可在局外随时查阅。 

随着你逐步收集齐殖民地遗失的音频与文本日志，偶然发现神秘涂鸦，深入探索废弃殖民地系统，并协助各阵营追寻各自的目标，一段迈向未知的旅程已然开启——而这也仅仅只是个开始。
