Music of Marathon — Dev Insights Video

Go behind-the-scenes for the making of Marathon’s music.

Marathon Dev Team February 13, 2026
community124 words1 min read

Explore how contemporary electronic influences and human performances blend together to create the music of Marathon, featuring audio director Chase Combs and composer Ryan Lott.

 

Four original tracks are now available for streaming on all major music streaming platforms.

Marathon (Original Soundtrack EP)

  • Rhythm of Writhing Constellations (2:25)
  • Focused Erasures and Sub-Routine Resets (1:22)
  • We Stole a Moon (2:02)
  • Code Race [Extended] (9:34)

We’re also excited to share that on March 6, you’ll be able to listen to the Marathon OST and score in its entirety on all major streaming platforms.

You can play Marathon in the Server Slam weekend from February 26, 2026 to March 2, 2026, available worldwide on Steam, PlayStation 5, and Xbox Series X|S.

Marathon releases March 5, 2026.

探索当代电子音乐与人类表演融合方式，创作《失落星船：马拉松》音乐，由音频总监Chase Combs与作曲家Ryan Lott倾力打造。

 

四首原声带现已在各大音乐流媒体平台上线。

失落星船：马拉松（原声带EP）

  • Rhythm of Writhing Constellations (2:25)
  • Focused Erasures and Sub-Routine Resets (1:22)
  • We Stole a Moon (2:02)
  • Code Race [Extended] (9:34)

我们也很高兴地宣布，3月7日起，你将能在所有主要流媒体平台收听完整《失落星船：马拉松》原声带和配乐。

你可以在2026年2月27日至3月3日的服务器压力测试周末期间游玩《失落星船：马拉松》。全球玩家均可在Steam、PlayStation 5和Xbox Series X|S平台体验。

《失落星船：马拉松》2026年3月6日发布。
